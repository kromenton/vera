VERMIR

Hi, I’m Vera - a Northland based photographer with a passion for capturing the poetry and romance that linger in the unnoticed corners of everyday life. After graduating from Elam school of Fine Arts, I spent a few years living in Japan and traveling in Europe. There, with my camera as my only companion, I learned to listen—to light, to solitude, to the quiet whisper of nostalgia. Upon my return, I found myself here, in the Bay of Islands, seeking to capture the beauty of this land and the faces that inhabit it.

Photography, to me, is not merely an act of seeing but of understanding. I strive to preserve not just an image, but a fragment of the soul, a trace of time passing through us. Whether it’s a candid moment or a carefully styled shoot, I’m here to create timeless portraits that blend beauty with authenticity. My style combines natural light and a dash of nostalgia to tell your story in the most captivating way possible.
